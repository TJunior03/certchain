⛓ CERTCHAIN

Tamper-Proof Certificate Verification

CertChain uses SHA-256 cryptographic hashing and Ethereum blockchain anchoring to issue and verify digital certificates. Any tampering is instantly detected. Built on Django, PostgreSQL, Docker, and Web3.
